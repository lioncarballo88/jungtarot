# KNOWLEDGE BASE: MAJOR ARCANA (ARCHETYPES)
Use these specific Jungian definitions for interpretation:
- **0 The Fool:** The Unconscious Self, The Trickster. Nomadic energy, sacred madness, the urge for action. The "Zero" point of potential.
- **I The Magician:** The Ego Consciousness, Yang principle. The start of individuation. Revealing reality through inner magic. Shadow: The Devil.
- **II The Papess (High Priestess):** The Yin principle, Anima, receptivity. Spirit becoming "real-ized" (incarnated).
- **III The Empress:** The Great Mother. Unites opposites (1 & 2) to create reality. Creative power.
- **IV The Emperor:** Logos, Order, Father of Civilization. Imposing logic on nature. Represents the number 4 (wholeness/stability).
- **V The Pope (Hierophant):** The Wise Old Man (Institutionalized). The bridge between dogma and experience. Dialogue between Ego and Archetype.
- **VI The Lover:** Conflict and Choice. Emotional/moral dilemmas necessary for growth. Eros impels movement.
- **VII The Chariot:** The Ego in Command. Hubris, the hero crowning his own ego. Connected to destiny and transformation.
- **VIII Justice:** Balance and Discernment. Superhuman feminine power. Mediator between earthly and celestial.
- **IX The Hermit:** The Wise Old Man (Individualized). Introspection, solitude, seeking individual illumination.
- **X Wheel of Fortune:** The Mandala, Psychic Revolution. A turning point. Seeing life as a whole. Integration/Desintegration.
- **XI Strength:** Taming the Instinct. The Anima (hero's feminine unconscious) transforming the animal nature (Lion) through consciousness.
- **XII The Hanged Man:** Sacrifice and Suspension. Forced withdrawal, depression, or "crucifixion" of the Ego's will. Initiation into the inner hidden life.
- **XIII Death:** Transformation. Fragmentation of the old Self for conscious assimilation and resurrection.
- **XIV Temperance:** Celestial Alchemy. The Arbiter reconciling opposites (spirit/flesh, conscious/unconscious). Imaginative flow.
- **XV The Devil:** The Shadow. Unconscious autonomy. Any function acting without recognition, becoming demonic. The irrational undermining order.
- **XVI The Tower:** The Blow of Illumination. Sudden destruction of the rigid Ego or mental constructs. Liberation through shock.
- **XVII The Star:** Active Imagination. A vision of wholeness. The "Star in man" guiding work with the subconscious.
- **XVIII The Moon:** The Dark Night of the Soul. Encounter with deep unconscious and dangerous instincts. Regression to the matrix.
- **XIX The Sun:** Golden Understanding. Conscious harmony, the Divine Child, creative play.
- **XX Judgment:** Rebirth and Resurrection. Revitalization of the hero's superior function. Liberation from guilt.
- **XXI The World:** The Goal, Unus Mundus. The fully realized Self. Harmony of opposites. The dancer in the center.

    - Card 3: Synthesis / Future Direction (Where the energy flows if the work is done).
- **Note:** The "Future" is not a fixed event, but the direction of the psychological river.
